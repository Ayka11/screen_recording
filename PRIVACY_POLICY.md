# Privacy Policy

Screen Recorder Pro respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Permissions requested are solely for app functionality:

- RECORD_AUDIO: Used to record audio during screen capture.
- MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Used to save recordings to your device.
- SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: Used for overlay controls.
- INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used for ads and connectivity checks.

No data is transmitted to external servers. For questions, contact the developer.
